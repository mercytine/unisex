The definition of unisex

英文释义：
unisex (adj.)
"sexually indistinguishable or neutral," 1968, from uni- + sex (n.).

About

现在，我们想定义unisex：男女皆宜的、不分性别的。
“unisex”作为虚拟 kol 试图打破性别界限， 倡导性别自由化，拓展审美边界，在框架之外“道德自由”地表达自我8个性和个体主张。通过以“无性别主义”主导的新话题、新潮流热点带动大众观念的新变化，呼唤着大众褪去超我的束缚，探寻本我与自我的真实表达。
“无性别”并不完全等同于“中性”，后者表述的是介于两者之间、没有明显性别特征的设计风格，前者则还多了各种设计师玩不腻的“雌雄同体”和“性别转0换”。在这里，种种性别特征明显的设计元素不再被框定在过去的性别选择之中，被重新组合、融造。

Now, unisex means "not distinguished on the basis of sex" and "regardless of gender". 
As a virtual KOL, "unisex" tries to break the gender boundary, advocate gender liberalization, expand the aesthetic boundary, and express self-personality and individual proposition "morally and freely" outside the framework. Through new topics and hot trends led by "non-sexism", it drives new changes in the public's ideas, calling for the public to shed the shackles of superego and explore the true expression of id and self.
The term "regardless of gender" is not exactly the same as "neutral," which refers to a design style that is somewhere in between and not overtly gender specific, while the term is filled with all manner of "androgynous" and "gender transposition," which designers are so used to. Here, various design elements with distinct gender characteristics are no longer framed in the past gender selection, but are recombined and fused today.

About

Agender

无性别者：亦称精神无性别者、精神无性别人，是一种性别酷儿或性别认同。无性别人认为他们没有性别或无关性别、没有感觉到自己有任何强烈性别归属的人。

Agender : also known as spiritually asexual or spiritually asexual, is a gender-queer or gender identity. A person who is identified by others as either genderless or gender-neutral, and who does not feel any strong gender identity.

LGBT+

LGBT+：性少数群体是指在性倾向、性别认同、性身份或性行为等方面上与社会上大多数人不同的群体，主要指的是性倾向非异性恋的群体、跨性别群体、非二元性别群体等。性少数群体是一个广泛的概念，即包含了媒体中常说的LGBT+人群，也包含了一些自我认同。

LGBT+ : The sexual minority group refers to the group that is different from the majority of people in the society in terms of sexual orientation, gender identity, sexual identity or sexual behavior, mainly refers to the group with sexual orientation of non-heterosexuality, transgender group, non-binary gender group, etc . Sexual minorities are a broad concept that encompasses what is often referred to in the media as LGBT+ people, as well as some self-identity.

Genderqueer

性别酷儿：近些年，随着“酷儿”一词在亚文化中的普及，出现了“性别酷儿”这一性别认同。通过将酷儿和性别结合在一起，自称“性别酷儿”的人强调自身对于“性别”这一概念和体制本身的批判和拒绝。性别认同为性别酷儿的人不愿被归类于任何一个既有性别分类。Ta们不认为自己是男性、女性、跨性别或间性人，反对性别体制对性别的划分。事实上，“性别酷儿”这一认同并没有本质的生物学基础，任何生理性别的人都可以自称自己是“性别酷儿”。换句话说，“性别酷儿”这一身份本身看似将“酷儿”固化成了一个身份，但其内部依然包含着上述“酷儿”一词所包含的双重性，即为了摧毁身份而存在的身份。

Genderqueer : In recent years, with the popularity of the word "queer" in the subculture, the gender identity of "genderqueer" has emerged. By combining queer and gender, people who call themselves "genderqueer" emphasize their criticism and rejection of the very concept of gender and the institution itself. People who identify as genderqueer do not want to fit into any of the established gender categories. They do not identify themselves as male, female, transgender or intersexual and reject the gender system's distinctions. In fact, the identity of "gender-queer" has no fundamental biological basis, and anyone of a biological gender can call themselves "gender-queer." In other words, the identity of "genderqueer" itself seems to solidify queer into an identity, but it still contains within it the duality that the word queer contains above, that is, identity exists for the purpose of destroying identity.
